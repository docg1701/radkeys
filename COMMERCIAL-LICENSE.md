## How to request a commercial license

Send an email to the repository author with the subject line **"RadKeys commercial
license request"** and include:

1. Company or individual name and country.
2. What you want to do (use internally, manufacture, resell, bundle, create a
   derivative, etc.).
3. Territory where you plan to operate.
4. Estimated volume or revenue in the first 12 months.
5. Whether you plan to sell the product as-is or as a modified derivative.

Typical commercial terms include a per-unit royalty or a revenue share, a
minimum annual guarantee, attribution requirements, and quality standards. Every
agreement is negotiated case by case.
